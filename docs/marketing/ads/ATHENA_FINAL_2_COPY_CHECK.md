## Best Hook

Use:

```text
$136/mo -> $9/mo
```

Why it works:

- Money is visible in the first second.
- It makes the ad feel like an ad, not only a cinematic brand film.
- It creates permission to explain context savings next.

Use this as the close:

```text
Stop paying AI to get lost.
Give your AI the repo map.
```

Why it works:

- Cost stays the main pain.
- It is short enough for video thumbnails.
- It explains the problem without jargon.
- It sets up the map metaphor cleanly.

Avoid:

```text
Your AI finally understands your entire codebase.
```

This is a good support line, but it is less scroll-stopping than the cost hook.

## Core RTB Order

1. Cost saved: `$136/mo -> $9/mo` on the 994-file benchmark.
2. Context saved: `453K -> 15.5K` tokens to understand the repo.
3. Repo learned fast: `0.5s` to map 994 files.
4. Check your repo: health check for dead code, broken links, and risk signals.

Accuracy is the downstream payoff, not the first RTB. Lead with savings, then show why the savings happen.
